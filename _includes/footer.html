Charity Partnerships

The Group will be supporting Jonathan’s Voice as its core charity. Jonathan’s Voice was set up shortly after Jon’s death in order to reduce the stigma associated with mental health issues and to educate organisations especially, but not exclusively, in the professional working environment to support employees and develop better mental health practices in the workplace.
